Welcome to Chatrapati Shivaji Travelers

Explore the Legacy of a Great Warrior King



Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire, is a symbol of bravery, valor, and strategic genius. Our travel service celebrates his rich heritage by offering immersive experiences that connect travelers to the history and culture of Maharashtra.



Discover Shivaji's Kingdom

Join us on a journey through the majestic forts, vibrant cities, and serene landscapes that Shivaji Maharaj once called home. Our carefully curated tours allow you to explore: Forts of Shivaji: Visit iconic forts such as Raigad, Pratapgad, and Torna, where you can learn about the strategic brilliance of Shivaji and his fight against oppression. Cultural Heritage: Experience the rich traditions, local cuisine, and folk performances that reflect the spirit of Maharashtra. Natural Beauty: Enjoy the breathtaking landscapes, from the Western Ghats to the Konkan coastline, ideal for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.



Tailored Travel Experiences

Whether you’re a history enthusiast, an adventure seeker, or a culture lover, we have something for everyone: Guided Tours: Expert guides will share stories and insights about Shivaji Maharaj and the Maratha Empire, enhancing your understanding of this historic region. Custom Packages: Create your own itinerary that caters to your interests, whether it’s trekking, photography, or historical exploration. Family-Friendly Options: We offer activities suitable for all ages, ensuring a memorable experience for the whole family.



Why Choose Us?


Authentic Experiences: We prioritize authenticity, ensuring that you experience the true essence of Maharashtra’s culture and history. Sustainable Tourism: We believe in responsible travel practices that support local communities and preserve the environment. Passionate Team: Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and ensuring your journey is seamless and enjoyable.
